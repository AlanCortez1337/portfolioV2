## Purpose

Everyone deserves a sweet treat which is why we have yummy bread to fill that void. But what many people forget is that there is a world of delicious savory bread. This is where this bakery Ecommerce proof of concept comes into play to demonstrate what it could be for a home bakery.

## Challenges

I will admit that I struggle with making modern and minimal designs because I love eccentric Neu-Brutalism with clashing colors. Another reason for the challenge is because it was challenging to add depth with just linear-gradients but I was able to address this by adding a textured background. After doing so it made the design feel unique and modern.
